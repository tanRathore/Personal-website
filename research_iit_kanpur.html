Research at IIT Kanpur

Conducting in-depth research on “Dependency Relations Knowledge Developed by LLMs” focused on exploring whether large language models utilize hierarchical dependency structures for word prediction, identifying the types of dependency relations learned, and analyzing their significance in model performance. Engaging in advanced research to explore the evolution of language based on cognitive constraints beyond working memory, examining why certain languages exhibit longer dependency lengths and investigating the reasons behind the variation in syntactic structures, such as SVO versus SOV configurations.
